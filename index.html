Open Robotics competition Egypt is a Summer Competition that first launched in 2016 to raise the awareness of the importance of robotics, programming, teamwork, and the spirit of honest competition, it is held under the patronage of the Arab Robotics Association ARA and qualifies Teams to participate in the Arab Robotics competition which is hosted every year by a different Arab country
